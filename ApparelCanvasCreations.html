We Customize Apparels

      involving Vinyl Heat Transfer and Image editing services that converts any images to be printable in vinyl.
Designs are transfered on a special film, that is placed on top of your garment.
A commercial heat press then applies heat to the film, which transfers the ink from the film to your garment.
The printing service can only use one color per vinyl, so requiring more than one color will require additional Vinyl per film/design

The heat press method is ideal for oddly-shaped garments, such as hats, backpacks, and fanny packs. It allows you to add logos in areas or on garments
